We have created a new way for people to make money by users sharing posts!. If a user shares a product post and someone from their network purchases the post, they'll receive a 4% sales commission. This mechanism will help sellers promote their products to broad networks!.

We provide sellers with 92% of their sales which is higher than several other marketplaces. We also provide you with a marketing platform where your products will be distributed through several networks. iMarkett’s marketing platform will accelerate your sales.

Can I make money without selling a product?

Yes! If you share products you love to your networks and someone from your networks makes a purchases what you shared. You'll earn a 4% commission!
